Web Design refers to the design of websites that are displayed on the internet. It is usually refers to the user experience aspects of website development ratherthan softwere develpment.... a webdesigner works on the appearence, layout , and in some cases, content of website.

What Web Designers Do ?

Web Design identifies the goals of a website or webpages and promotes accessibility for all potential users. this process involves organising content and images across a series of pages, integrating applications and their interactive elements.

The Professionals who perform this process are called webdesigners, and their jobs include following duties:

  • Selecting easy-to-read fonts.
  • Choosing Attractive Color Schemes that also enable easy-to-read fonts.
  • implementing a brand's identity into the colors,fonts and layouts.
  • Creating a map of website structure to ensure intuitive navigation.
  • Placing images,logos , text , image , appliations and other ekements.
  • Using Coding languages such as HTML , CSS , to create layout and style pages.
  • making optimized versions of websites and pages both for desktop and mobile viewing.
